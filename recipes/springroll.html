Spring Roll Recipe

Vietnamese Spring Rolls

Refreshing vietnamese spring roll made of noodles, shrimp, pork, and various vegetables wrapped in rice paper

Ingredients

    - 2 ounces of vermicelli noodles

    - Peeled and deveined cooked shrimp

    - Lettuce (and any other vegetables you want to add)

    - Cooked and thiny sliced pork loin or belly

    - Rice paper

    - Peanut sauce made of water, peanut butter, hoison sauce, and chili paste

Directions

    1. Fill a large pot with water and bring to boil

    2. Add in vermicilli noodles and cook until tender

    3. Fill a large bowl with warm water to dip rice paper and soften

    4. Place softened rice paper on plate and add on noodles, pork, shrimp, and vegetables in a row

    5. Fold uncovered sides inward and then tightly roll the wrapper, beginning at the lettuce end

    6. Dip roll in peanut sauce and enjoy
