Salmon Sashimi

Spicy salmon sashimi in ponzu sauce

Ingredients

    - 1 lb sushi grade salmon

    - 2 tbsp ponzu sauce

    - 1 tbsp soy sauce

    - 1 tsp sesame oil

    - 1-2 jalapeno chilis

Directions

    1. Slice salmon into thin sashimi pieces and lay peices flat onto plate

    2. Clean japaleno and slice jalapeno into small, circular peices

    3. Place each sliced jalapeno piece onto a sashimi salmon

    4. Take a small bowl and mix together ponzu sauce, sesame oil, and soy sauce

    5. Carefully pour the sauce mixture over the sashimi pieces

    6. Enjoy with rice or seaweed paper (optional)
