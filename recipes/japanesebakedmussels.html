Japanese Baked Mussels

Mussels baked to perfection with creamy spicy mayo and masago

Ingredients

    - 1 dozen green New Zealand mussels

    - 1 cup kewpie mayonnaise

    - 2 teaspoons sriracha

    - 1 teaspoon lemon juice

    - 2 tablespoons masago

    - 1 stalk scallion sliced (green part only)

Directions

    1. Rinse and pat mussels dry, leaving on the shell and lining them up on a baking sheet

    2. Mix together sriracha, kewpie mayonnaise, and lemon juice in a bowl

    3. Take a small spoonful of this mixture and add it on top of the mussels

    4. Heat oven to 350 degrees F; put in mussels and let bake for 15-20 minutes

    5. Once done baking, take mussels out and add a small abount of masago on top of each mussel

    6. Garnish with masago and scallions
